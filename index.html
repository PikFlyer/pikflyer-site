Frequently asked questions

Is Pik Flyer safe to use?

Pik Flyer simulates GPS location at the operating system level. It works with Pikmin Bloom and other location-based apps. As with any location tool, please use responsibly and be aware of each app's terms of service.

What devices are supported?

iPhone (iOS 17+) and Android (5.0+). Your phone connects to Pik Flyer via USB on a Mac or Windows computer.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Your subscription can be cancelled at any time from your account dashboard. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, new users get a 3-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.

Do I need to jailbreak my iPhone?

No. Pik Flyer uses Apple's Developer Tools protocol to simulate location. No jailbreak or modifications to your device are needed.
