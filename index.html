Frequently asked questions 常見問題

Is Pik Flyer safe to use? Pik Flyer 安全嗎？

Pik Flyer simulates GPS location at the operating system level. It works with Pikmin Bloom and other location-based apps. Please use responsibly and be aware of each app's terms of service. Pik Flyer 在作業系統層級模擬 GPS 定位，適用於 Pikmin Bloom 及其他定位型 App。請負責任地使用，並注意各 App 的服務條款。

What devices are supported? 支援哪些裝置？

iPhone (iOS 17+) and Android (5.0+). Your phone connects to Pik Flyer via USB on a Mac or Windows computer. iPhone (iOS 17+) 和 Android (5.0+)。手機透過 USB 連接到 Mac 或 Windows 電腦上的 Pik Flyer。

Can I cancel anytime? 可以隨時取消嗎？

Yes. Cancel at any time from your account dashboard. You keep access until the end of your billing period. 可以，隨時從帳戶後台取消。取消後仍可使用至帳單週期結束。

Do I need to jailbreak my iPhone? 需要越獄嗎？

No. Pik Flyer uses Apple's Developer Tools protocol to simulate location. No jailbreak or modifications needed. 不用。Pik Flyer 使用 Apple 開發者工具協定模擬定位，不需要越獄或任何改機。

How smooth is the auto walk? 自動走路順暢嗎？

Pik Flyer uses a dual-thread architecture that sends continuous GPS signals for ultra-smooth movement. No jerky stops — your character walks naturally. Pik Flyer 使用雙線程架構，連續發送 GPS 訊號實現超平滑移動。不會走走停停，角色自然行走。
