Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you purchase a subscription, our payment partner (Lemon Squeezy) collects your name, email address, and payment information. We receive your email address and license key for account management purposes. We do not collect or store your payment card details directly.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your email address solely to manage your subscription, send license keys, and provide customer support. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data the Software Collects

Pik Flyer runs entirely on your local computer. The Software does not collect, transmit, or store your GPS coordinates, location history, device identifiers, or any usage data. All location simulation happens locally on your machine.

4. License Verification

The Software periodically contacts the Lemon Squeezy API to verify your subscription status. This communication includes only your license key and does not transmit any personal data or location information.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not use cookies, analytics trackers, or any third-party tracking scripts.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. All communications between the Software and our licensing server use HTTPS encryption.

7. Data Retention

If you cancel your subscription, we retain your email and license information for up to 12 months for support purposes, after which it is deleted.

8. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Pik Flyer is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

11. Contact
